A little about me

Ask me annie-thing!

I am an altruistic, creative soul, always striving to lend a hand. My background in healthcare alongside my undergraduate education in biology has allowed me to further my passion for helping others.

My previous experience in dentistry has broadened my perspective of what it means to contribute to my community. While dentistry and UI/UX are quite different as some would say, I believe that, ultimately, they embody the same foundation: helping those in need, whether it be through patient care or user experience.

As I transition into the design field, I hope to utilize my intuitive and innovative nature to create digital experiences beyond boundaries!
